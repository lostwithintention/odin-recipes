Lasagna recipe

Why lasagna?

Lasagna is one of many delicious recipes from the italian kitchen, it consist of a meat sauce (even better with leftover bolognese) and cheese sauce that are put layered with pasta plates.

Ingredients

Meat sauce:

  • 400 g mincemeat
  • 2 tbs oil for frying
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic chopped
  • 1 hermetic box of chopped tomato (400g)
  • 2 tbs tomato puree
  • About 1 dl water
  • 1 tbs fresh basil
  • 0,5 teaspoon salt
  • o,5 teaspoon pepper

Cheese sauce:

  • 2 tbs butter
  • 3 tbs flour
  • 6 dl milk
  • 5 tbs grated parmesan or another white cheese
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 0,5 teaspoon pepper (white if you have)
  • 0,5 grated nutmeg
  • 9 pieces of lasagna plates
  • 3 dl grated white cheese

Steps

  1. Split the mincemeat in two, and brown in the oil on high heat. Put it all back into the frying pan, Add the chopped onion, garlic, tomato, tomato puree and the water. Let it simmer for about 10 minutes until it starts to thicken. Put the basil in. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Melt butter in a pot and stir in the flour. Slowly pour in the milk while stirring, and let the sauce boil for 10 minutes. It should be thick. Add the parmesan and let the cheese melt. Season the cheese sauce.
  3. Add lasagna plates, neat sauce and cheese sauce layered in a ovenproof container. Start and end with the cheese sauce. At the top layer add the grated white cheese.
  4. Put the oven on 225 Celsius and cook for 30-40min. Check with something sharp (knife) if the pasta is soft. Let the lasagna rest for a few minutes before serving.
